three year old program

Yellow Room and Purple Room

Our three year old program is designed with active three year olds in mind. Threes are naturally curious and highly imaginative. In a positive environment, we work to develop and strengthen self-help skills, cooperation, interpersonal communication, and fine and gross motor skills. In addition to basic learning concepts (letters, numbers, colors, shapes) we provide a foundation of Christ centered learning, with many stories, lessons, and songs.
