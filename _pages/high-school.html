high school ministry

Connect. Explore God. Have fun.

At the HS Weekly, 9-12th graders can be themselves, build community and have authentic conversations about God. You don't have to have be a Bible expert. You don't have to have the answers. You don't even have to be sure what you believe. You're welcome at the Weekly.
