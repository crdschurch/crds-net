SERVE IN KIDS' CLUB

Sign up for Kids' Club and you’ll get to help make room for new kids by greeting families, helping toddlers feel comfortable in their bubble-filled rooms, or leading a small group of kids in simple activities that help them feel God’s love. We’ll pair you with an experienced Kids’ Club volunteer—and you’ll have a blast.

Sign up to be a fabulous first impression at Crossroads. You can help park cars, welcome people through the doors, and guide people to open seats. Super rewarding, super easy.
