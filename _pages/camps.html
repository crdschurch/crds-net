Free of distractions and noise. Free of timelines and daily grind.

Woman Camp
April 27-28, 2019 (tickets available now)

An overnight camping experience for women to go to new places with God, receive their God-given identity, and find belonging in community. We’ll be cooking over open fires, praying under trees, singing outside, setting up tables, sleeping in tents, pouring out water and wine, and laughing 'til morn.
