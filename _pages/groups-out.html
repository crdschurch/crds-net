group resources: out

The early Church added people daily. Being in a Group provides a place to invite our friends who don’t know Jesus, since they may find it easier to hang out with you and your friends than coming directly to a church building. Growing in OUT looks like doing activities that allow a seeker to jump in with your group. It’s being aware of the work God is already doing in the people around you, and stepping into partnership with him.

These resources can help grow your group’s OUT.

Follow: Someone who’s all in, ready to stop negotiating with God and follow wherever He leads.

Produce: Someone with a history of making other disciple-makers, whose life looks like Jesus with a depth of character, competence and fruitfulness.
