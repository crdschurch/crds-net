At Crossroads Andover

Crossroads Preschool provides a safe, fun and welcoming environment where kids can grow and develop intellectually, physically, socially, and emotionally through cognitive experiences and hands on activity. Music, art and play are used to encourage creative expression. Staff is trained to use positive management techniques which encourages children to set internal limits on his/her own behavior.

More details about Crossroad's Preschool:

  • Classes are 9am to 12pm.
  • We offer two day classes for 2 year olds.
  • Two day and three day classes for 3 year olds.
  • Two, three and five day classes for 4-year-olds through Pre-Kindergarteners.
If you are interested in registering for Crossroads Preschool please contact Sommer Anderson at 859-618-6046 or sommer.anderson@crossroads.net. We currently have a few spots available for our 2018-2019 Tuesday, Thursday 3s and 4s classes. You can also be added to our waitlists for our other classes. Pre-registration for 2019-2020 (current families) begins Jan. 10. Open Registration begins Jan 24.
