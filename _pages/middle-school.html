middle school ministry

Connect. Explore God. Have fun.

The MSM Weekly is a place for 6-8th graders to hang out, build relationships and engage in God's story. We have a tricked-out game room, tons of opportunities to make lasting friendship, and an experience—with teaching and breakout groups—specially designed for students to explore God.
