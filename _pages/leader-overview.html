way to step up and lead a group

Leading a group is a big deal. Leaders aren’t just interested in finding friends. You’re moving people closer to God with the vision he’s given you for your group, and by caring about each person you lead. You don’t have to be a spiritual guru, just interested in following God yourself and helping others do the same.

And if there’s anything else you need—just ask! We’d love to make it happen.
