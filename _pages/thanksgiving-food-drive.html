Thanksgiving Food Drive

God says to serve those in need. So we do.

Commitment: One-time serve
Perfect for: Groups, Families, Adults

We believe that helping those in need is a core part of who we are and who God wants us to be. So we do it in a big, big way at Thanksgiving. This year, we've ordered thousands of boxes, expectant that God will show up through your unprecedented generosity.

Both locally and nationally, in partnership with churches and faith-based ministries, we'll be providing Thanksgiving meals to over 115,000 people blessing those in our communities. We’ll also be sending 60,000 pounds of food to our partners in South Africa.

Be a part of our 23rd annual Thanksgiving Food Drive by signing up to serve during box collection. We'll collect the filled boxes that will then be shared with partners and ultimately to tables throughout our communities. If you enjoy the honking of car horns, this job is especially for you.
