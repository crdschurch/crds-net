we'd love to meet you

Prayer Appointment
Would you like to schedule some prayer time with one of our volunteers? Email communitycare@crossroads.net

Listening Appointment
A listening appointment pairs you with one of our trained community care volunteers who will combine a time of listening and reflection as well as prayer. At the end of this time we can also provide you with names of counselors or other groups that might meet your needs. Email communitycare@crossroads.net

Rooted Prayer Sessions
Our team facilitates a process that helps you with the following:

  • Listen to your life for greater clarity and insight
  • Listen to God as he speaks with you about the roots of painful emotions or behaviors
  • Respond to God’s invitation to address the root issues blocking freedom and health
  • Receive grace and truth to renew your mind, emotions, and body

To make an appointment, email rootedprayermason@crossroads.net
