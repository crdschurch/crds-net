crossroads weekday preschool

four year old program

Orange and Gray Room

Our goals in this program are to prepare our students for kindergarten, give them a love for learning, and to show them that Jesus loves them. We use a structured, consistent, and loving environment to help each child reach his/her potential. We use a variety of large and small motor activities, music, art, and games to keep the children engaged and to help them to master skills like letter sounds, patterns, number recognition, etc.
