CityLink

Commitment: High
Perfect for: Adults

Volunteers are actively impacting our community at CityLink: One place with One mission.

Through engagement, lives of clients and volunteers will change as we show those in poverty that they are valued and valuable.

CityLink volunteers dramatically increase the capacity of clients engaging at the Center and its partners to address our pressing community need and help those in need overcome poverty in our city. 

Clients come to ONE place and are surrounded by ONE team made up of many of the city’s best services and have volunteers and staff walk alongside them to achieve their goals.

We understand that our clients are a whole person, that one aspect of their life affects another, so we want to help with all aspects of their life, providing an integrated approach for lasting impact.

CityLink has 22 roles with hundreds of volunteers serving in different capacities, frequencies, and durations. With options from mock interviews to auto care, tutoring to gardening, clothing connection to resume review, there is literally something for everyone.

The skills and talents you take for granted can make a real and tangible difference in the life of someone in our community. We provide the platform, volunteers bring the time and talent to make the impact.
