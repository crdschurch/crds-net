CONTINUE TO PRAY

We’re praying for the mega themes our leadership team feels are our wildly important goals (WIGS) for the upcoming ministry year:

  • Attract - we want more people – the people we love – to come to know God
  • Connect - we want the people we attract to build relationships in community
  • Transform - we want the people who become connected to be transformed to live generous and free lives
