AT CROSSROADS WORSHIP TRAINING
You’ll be getting hands-on experience.

We believe that everyone has the potential to be great. That’s why we’ve created Worship Training. Whether a musician or technician, whether behind a camera or a drumset, you’ll get reps on our stages with our gear, basically like being a part of a band or production crew.

AT CROSSROADS WORSHIP TRAINING
You’ll get personalized instruction.

You’ll get personalized feedback from someone who is doing what you want to be doing - playing guitar, running a camera, mixing at the soundboard, whatever. Our goal is to see your skills grown to their full potential. We’ll provide the training. You provide the desire and the hard work.

MUSIC

Music is powerful. It has the ability to captivate, move and mobilize people. We want to hone our musical gifts to a fine edge, so that we can communicate God’s love for people as beautifully and forcefully as possible.

PRODUCTION

Our goal is to create the immersive environments at all of our sites. Lights, cameras, LED walls, lyrics, thundering sounds, etc. If you like fast-paced professional quality, we’re looking for you. Don’t have any experience? No worries, we’ll train you.

THE PROCESS

STEP 1
LET US GET TO KNOW YOU

Tell us who you are, and a little bit about your experience. If you’re a technician, we’ll follow up with a brief interview to see where best to place you. If a musician, we’ll invite you to a low-stress audition to get a better sense of your abilities and style. We'll give you everything you need to crush it.

STEP 2
SUBMIT YOUR VIDEO AUDITION

We want to hear (and see) you in action. We'll give you the tools you need to have a great, stress-free audition.

STEP 3
TRAIN

Weekly training serves as our way to invest in our team, and you. You'll hone your skills onstage and off, growing your gifts and abilities. Our hope is that as you grow and develop you'll be invited to put them to use creating beautiful, compelling experiences throughout our community.
