Ask Me Who Loves Me

This music is great for parents who want to remind their kids who they are, who made them and who has a plan for them. It's also great for kids who want to change the world and won't stop until they do. Use it for spontaneous dance parties, worshipping God with friends and family and inviting your friends to come to Kids' Club with you!
