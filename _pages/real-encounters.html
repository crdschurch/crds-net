Real Encounters takes you on an adventure through Israel to see the real-life places you read about in the Bible. Explore stories of ancient people and their experiences with God. You’ll also hear from people in our communities who are encountering God today.

About Season 2:
This season we take a look at battlegrounds. Every day we’re confronted with things that want to trip us up, weigh us down, and knock us out of the fight. We’re back in Israel to learn from the real stories of ancient warriors who fought, bled, won, lost, and encountered God all along the way. Just like for them, people today are encountering God and winning their battles.

39 mins 8 sec

Episode 1: Training

See the site where Paul was held prisoner and wrote about the concept of training. Hear from Alex who is in a battle to save his business.

40 mins 45 sec

Episode 2: Trust

In Israel, we look at the story of Deborah, who led Israel in their struggle against the Canaanites, and the role of female leaders in the biblical story. Leah shares her battle with addiction.

41 mins 40 sec

Episode 3: Aggression

Dive into the story of biblical hero Joshua, and how godly aggression can determine success or failure. Hear from a group of people who marched around a building, praying for a miracle.

43 mins 40 sec

Episode 4: Repentance

Explore the story of David on location in Israel. Brandy and Jon share their story of repentance and the battle to save their marriage.

48 mins 06 sec

Episode 5: Humility

The final episode of season two starts at the Sea of Galilee. We examine Jesus, the world’s greatest warrior ever. Freddy talks about battling his personal demons.

About Season 1:
Season one features stories of people willing to risk, explore, and face the unknown. They took journeys that revealed their true identity, forged their character, and brought them to a place of freedom.

46 mins 20 sec

Episode 1: Find Relief

Begin on a climb up Mt. Carmel following the story of Elijah. Hear Alison’s story about how God showed up to bring her relief in the face of tragedy.

41 mins 27 sec

Episode 2: Find Comfort

In the city of Capernaum, then in the sea of Galilee, hear stories of Jesus bringing comfort in the face of illness, emptiness and guilt. Michael talks about how roots brought him to his knees and led to a real experience with God.

36 mins 20 sec

Episode 3: Find Transformation

See where David and Goliath battled, then explore a cave where David hit an all-time low. Will’s abusive childhood makes it hard for him to be a husband and father. In both stories, God shows up and makes incredible transformations.

47 mins 36 sec

Episode 4: Find Healing

In Jeruselum, trace the exact footsteps of a sick man in search of healing. Jamie and Crystal share how God showed up for them with a huge miracle. Robbie shares what it’s like when affliction persists.

53 mins 29 sec

Episode 5: Find Freedom

At the oldest border in the world, the supposed “cursed” land of Tel Dan, learn what it means to break free from the things that weigh us down. Michael’s life is crashing around him. Hear how he experiences God and finds freedom from his past.
