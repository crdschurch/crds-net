If you are a parent wanting to make a payment toward your student's camp account, PLEASE do not use this page. 

Go directly to crossroads.net/mycamps, login to your account and click the "make a payment" button. Seriously, we will all be glad you did.

If you want to help give a student or two or three or more a great camp experience then you are in the right place!

Camp is fun and life-changing and your support enables more students to experience it.

Please note that payments for student camp are not tax-deductible. Thank you for your generosity!

If you wish to support more than one student, please enter separate payments for each student. You can designate a specific student to receive your support, by entering their name in the appropriate box on the next page of this form. 

If you would like to support the general camp fund, please enter the Crossroads Student Ministry Site you would like to support.
