Christmas Gift Drive

The Academy

Commitment: High
Perfect for: Adults

Change a child's life. Help at-risk kids grow, succeed, and lead through tutoring, mentoring, and enrichment. As their self value radically improves, they begin to feel hope. That’s how lives change. From weekly to as-needed, the time commitments are as varied as the roles.

Learn more about The Academy

Bloc Ministries

Commitment: Low-High
Perfect for: Adults, Women, Groups, Students

BLOC Ministry lives and works with the people of the streets in Price Hill and Cleves area. We live and work in these areas in order to be the best neighbor we can possibly be and to share the hope that we are far greater than our worst mistakes.

Learn more about Bloc Ministries

CityLink Center

Commitment: High

Volunteers are actively impacting our community at CityLink: One place with One mission. Through engagement, lives of clients and volunteers will change as we show those in poverty that they are valued and valuable.

eWIT

Commitment: High
Perfect for: Adults (18+)

No fancy skills needed. You don’t have to be a teacher or a technology genius to help change the lives of our students in India/Nepal. Join us in helping students on the other side of the globe increase their opportunities and secure their futures of freedom by improving their English skills and preparing them for secondary education and gainful employment.

Learn more about eWIT

Go Local

Commitment: One-time serve
Perfect for: Everybody

People with muscles, people who can organize, people who love to get dirty, and people who just love their city need to be a part of GO Local. In four hours on a weekend in May, we tackle over 700 projects at schools, social agencies, parks, neighborhoods, and churches all over town.

Learn more about GO Local
