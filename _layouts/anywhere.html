Connect in person

Find people near you.

Church is not a building. It started with a bunch of people who were literally following Jesus around, eating together, and learning to do what He did. People were included, healed, changed, and connected.

It’s way more accessible than we realize. Whether you’re new to God and just looking for some better people to be around or you’re actively following Jesus, let’s connect.

Eat together

What do church and eating together have in common? According to Jesus, a lot. He was the life of the party and he never missed an opportunity to turn a meal into something more.

For centuries, church happened around a table. Eating together is one of the simplest ways to copy Jesus’ life. That thing we do 3x a day already, what if we did it more intentionally?

Eat with whoever you live with, and invite others to join. Create community out of thin air. Reach out to our team for more coaching on how to handle kids, find people, make it better, and more.

About

Crossroads Anywhere

Following Jesus is not a list of dos and don’ts. It’s an invitation to a spiritual adventure that’s way bigger than Sunday mornings.

We just keep asking: what if the people who followed Jesus actually looked like Him?

What if we were the most loving people on earth? What if we moved in His power? What if we had fun and were inclusive like He was? What if we brought justice like He did? That’s what we’re after, and we’d love for you to join us—whoever you are, wherever you are.

New Here?

What to expect

We want the kind of church that goes way beyond the walls of a building and shows up IRL. We meet in-person and online, in homes, coffee shops, breweries, in our app—anywhere that people are connecting to follow Jesus together.

We’re not a content channel or Christian influencers. We’re regular humans with our own problems who found real freedom in Jesus. And we will do anything short of sin to help others do the same.

No matter where you live, you can join us. No matter what you believe, you’re welcome.

Take the Wayfinder

Not sure where you are with God or how to get started? Take the Wayfinder—a quiz to identify where you are spiritually and how to get moving again.

Grow with us

We believe there’s a path to the full life Jesus promises. Practices He modeled that we’re all trying to follow together. Join us in time-tested, data-proven practices to grow your faith and actually get some breakthrough.
