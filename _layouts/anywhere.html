About

Crossroads Anywhere

Following Jesus is not a list of dos and don’ts. It’s an invitation to a spiritual adventure that’s way bigger than Sunday mornings.

We just keep asking: what if the people who followed Jesus actually looked like Him?

What if we were the most loving people on earth? What if we moved in His power? What if we ate together and had fun and were inclusive like He was? What if we brought justice like He did? That’s what we’re after, and we’d love for you to join us—wherever you are.

New Here?

What to expect

We want the kind of church that goes way beyond the walls of a building and shows up IRL. We meet in-person and online, in homes, coffee shops, breweries, in our app—anywhere that people are connecting to follow Jesus together.

We’re not a content channel or Christian influencers. We’re regular humans with our own problems who found real freedom in Jesus. And we will do anything short of sin to help others do the same.

No matter where you live, you can join us. No matter what you believe, you’re welcome.
